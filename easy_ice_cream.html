Easy Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • your choice of flavor and additional ingredients

Directions

Add 1 pint of heavy whipping cream to a bowl.
Mix with a hand mixer until soft peaks are formed.
Add 1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk to the heavy whipping cream.
Mix with a hand mixer until the mixture is think and well-combined.
Add whatever flavor or additional ingredients you choose. (Ex. chocolate chips or a vanilla bean)
Freeze mixture for at least 6 hours.

Enjoy!
